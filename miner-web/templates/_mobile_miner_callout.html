Android

  1. Open Web Miner in Chrome
  2. Tap ⋮ → Install app or Add to Home screen
  3. Launch from your home screen — runs fullscreen with miner_kind=android pool tracking

Native APK (v{{ run_locally.android_miner_version if run_locally else '1.3.5' }}) can run pruned, full chain, or Time Capsule local nodes (no wallet on device). Time Capsule syncs a pruned tip and backs up archived history across devices. Enable “Install unknown apps” to sideload.

iPhone / iPad

  1. Open Web Miner in Safari (not Chrome)
  2. Tap Share → Add to Home Screen
  3. Keep the app in the foreground while mining — iOS limits background CPU

Apple does not allow mining apps in the App Store. Home-screen install (PWA) is the supported path on iOS.

Pool mode on phone earns proportional STONE and may receive a share of ASIC block subsidies. Use Wi‑Fi, keep the device cool, and expect low hashrate vs desktop — every phone helps spread the load.
