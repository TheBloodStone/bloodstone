3 · Swap STONE (after listing)

  1. Update GleecDEX (or legacy Komodo Wallet) to the latest release — coin list updates ship with app updates.
  2. Open DEX and search for STONE or STONE-segwit.
  3. Deposit STONE to the wallet's STONE address (from the web wallet or your own node).
  4. Choose a counterparty coin (e.g. BTC, KMD, LTC) and create an atomic swap order.
  5. Wait for confirmations (~6 blocks). Bloodstone uses Electrum servers once listed — no account on this portal is required.

Until listing completes, you can still send/receive STONE via the web wallet or a synced full node.
